ABOUT ME

My name is Sydney O'Connor, and I am a fourth year student at the University of Cincinnati, pursuing my B.S. in Computer Engineering and Master of Engineering in Computer Science. I was born and raised in the Cincinnati area and have always been a huge Bearcats fan. When I first came to UC, I had no idea what I wanted to do with my life. I was introduced to programming in my freshman courses and found a passion for computer engineering. Now, I've had the opportunity to do three co-ops and connect my passions to my field of study. I love supporting and encouraging women in technology through my involvement in ACM-W, CEAS Ambassadors, the Student Alumni Council, CWEST and Sigma Phi.
